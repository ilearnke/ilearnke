The story

Running a music career or label is hard work, and time consuming. There’s distribution, marketing, accounting, keeping your team informed, calculating royalties, and most crucially, making sure your music gets heard by people other than your best friends. Revelator wants to use technology and smart software to make performing all these (mostly admin-like) tasks a bit less tedious and a lot more enjoyable and insightful.


Their platform lets anyone, from the just-starting-out to the major label, get one holistic dashboard view of all the transactions happening in a business - from sales, marketing, and reporting, to analytics. Is your song getting played? If so, who is listening? Did that marketing campaign you publish really work? Revelator gives you the insights and answers to these types of questions, along with cold hard numbers - data - to back it up and help you make your next big move.

Our team traveled to Israel to meet with the Revelators in person. Over the course of 2 days we hammered out some USPs and benefits, and zeroed in on the company’s core values and purpose to help establish a clear messaging framework. We then crafted a new logo and supporting identity system, and dove headfirst into their existing product design and UX, working to refine the usability and overall design of the system. To coincide with SXSW 2015, we launched a marketing site to help sell the overall experience of using this super smart software to help make music businesses better.
